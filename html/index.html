Game Rules

  1. Roll the Dice: At the beginning of each round, the player rolls two dice.
  2. Check for Ones: If either of the dice shows a 1, the score for that round is 0.
    • Example: If the dice show 6 and 1, the score is 0.
  3. Check for Pairs: If both dice show the same number, the score is the sum of the two dice multiplied by 2.
    • Example: If the dice show 5 and 5, the score is (5+5) * 2 = 20.
  4. Calculate Other Scores: If the roll does not meet the criteria of the first two rules, the score is the sum of the two dice.
    • Example: If the dice show 3 and 2, the score is 3+2 = 5.
  5. Repeat for Each Round: These rules apply to each round of the game, and the player's score for the round is added to their total score.
